# Contact

The AfriGen-D fork of Imputation Server 2 is maintained alongside the
upstream codebase at [`genepi/imputationserver2`](https://github.com/genepi/imputationserver2).

## Upstream maintainers

The upstream Imputation Server 2 project is developed by the
[Institute of Genetic Epidemiology](https://genepi.i-med.ac.at/) at the
Medical University of Innsbruck:

- [Lukas Forer](https://genepi.i-med.ac.at/team/forer-lukas/)
- [Sebastian Schönherr](https://genepi.i-med.ac.at/team/schoenherr-sebastian/)

## AfriGen-D fork

For African-cohort-specific reference panels, deployment, and the
`impute.afrigen-d.org` service:

- Service homepage: [impute.afrigen-d.org](https://impute.afrigen-d.org)
- Service documentation: [imputation-service-docs](https://afrigen-d.github.io/imputation-service-docs/)
- AfriGen-D consortium: [afrigen-d.org](https://afrigen-d.org)
- Issue tracker for this fork: [GitHub issues](https://github.com/AfriGen-D/imputationserver2/issues)
